YouTube-VIS Annotation Overlay (2-video approach)

This demo expects two local videos from one YouTube-VIS sequence: the original RGB video and a rendered annotation-overlay video.

Overlay: YouTube-VIS annotation video
Put files here: /static/examples/assets/ytvos/base.mp4 and /static/examples/assets/ytvos/overlay-mask.mp4
Example ffmpeg from YouTube-VOS frames: 
ffmpeg -framerate 30 -i JPEGImages/<seq>/%05d.jpg -c:v libx264 -pix_fmt yuv420p base.mp4
ffmpeg -framerate 30 -i Annotations/<seq>/%05d.png -vf colorkey=black:0.15:0.05 -c:v libx264 -pix_fmt yuv420p overlay-mask.mp4
